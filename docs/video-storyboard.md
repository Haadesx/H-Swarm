# Video Storyboard

## Video Goal

Create a short founder-led product video that feels like an enterprise healthcare product demo, not an AI experiment.

Recommended length:

- teaser: 45 to 60 seconds
- product demo: 2.5 to 3 minutes

## Structure

### Scene 1: Problem

On screen:

- title card
- healthcare finance / operations visuals
- product name

Voiceover:

> When reimbursement drops, health systems do not just lose margin.  
> They face denial pressure, staffing strain, access deterioration, and lender scrutiny at the same time.

### Scene 2: Current Failure

On screen:

- fragmented spreadsheets
- policy memo
- KPI export
- financing snapshot

Voiceover:

> Today, these decisions are made across disconnected decks, spreadsheets, and one-off analysis.  
> Operators, boards, lenders, and investors end up working from different versions of the truth.

### Scene 3: Product Introduction

On screen:

- HealthTwin Projects screen
- `Import MetroCare Demo`

Voiceover:

> HealthTwin turns a healthcare evidence pack into a structured decision run.

### Scene 4: Assumption Review

On screen:

- assumptions
- facts
- KPI baseline

Voiceover:

> It begins by grounding the case in assumptions, KPI baselines, and evidence-backed facts.

### Scene 5: Stakeholder Map

On screen:

- stakeholder matrix
- conflict view

Voiceover:

> Then it shows who is exposed, what each stakeholder optimizes for, and where the pressure will surface first.

### Scene 6: Scenario Lab

On screen:

- base / downside / severe
- heatmap
- timeline

Voiceover:

> Instead of static reporting, HealthTwin compares 30, 90, and 180-day paths across operations, access, and financing risk.

### Scene 7: Memo Workspace

On screen:

- Operator Brief
- Capital Memo

Voiceover:

> One run generates two synchronized outputs: one for operators, one for capital stakeholders.

### Scene 8: Why It Matters

On screen:

- concise benefits

Voiceover:

> This is how health systems move from fragmented evidence to faster, more aligned decisions.

## Recording Notes

- use the deterministic MetroCare demo
- avoid live typing during the main cut
- zoom in on KPI and memo panels
- keep cursor movement slow and deliberate
- use clean browser chrome

## Final Title Card

> HealthTwin  
> Evidence to action for healthcare operators and capital stakeholders
