# Product Overview

## Positioning

HealthTwin is a healthcare decision workspace for provider operators and capital stakeholders.
It is built for scenarios where financial, operational, access, and financing pressures interact,
and where leadership teams need a faster way to move from raw evidence to aligned action.

The first wedge is reimbursement stress and covenant defense for multi-site providers.

## Who It Is For

- provider CFO teams
- strategy and transformation offices
- turnaround leaders
- lender and sponsor teams with provider exposure
- healthcare diligence teams

## What It Produces

Each run is designed to generate a board-usable decision package:

- grounded assumptions
- KPI baseline
- stakeholder conflict view
- scenario comparisons across 30, 90, and 180 days
- `Operator Brief`
- `Capital Memo`

## What It Is Not

HealthTwin is not:

- a social simulation tool
- a diagnosis or treatment system
- a trading or investment recommendation engine
- a generic graph demo

## Product Philosophy

The product is intentionally structured around evidence control, workflow discipline, and buyer clarity.
Graphs are available, but the primary product artifact is the decision package itself.

## Commercial Story

The acquisition story is straightforward:

- providers need faster financial-operating decision support
- existing healthcare workflow systems do not package this work cleanly
- capital stakeholders want the same evidence base translated into their own language

That creates a natural fit for acquirers in revenue cycle, operations, analytics, and healthcare finance software.
