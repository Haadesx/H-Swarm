# MetroCare Health System Board Brief

MetroCare Health System operates two regional hospitals, six outpatient clinics, and a physician network serving a mixed Medicare and commercial payer population across the Mid-Atlantic region.

## Current operating profile

- Medicare represents 41% of gross patient revenue.
- Commercial payer contracts represent 38% of gross patient revenue.
- Medicaid and other governmental programs represent 16%.
- Self-pay and other categories represent 5%.

## Pressure points

- Labor inflation remains above budget across nursing and care coordination teams.
- Denials have increased in musculoskeletal, cardiovascular, and post-acute episodes.
- Covenant headroom has narrowed after two quarters of margin compression.
- Referral leakage to a lower-cost competitor has increased in orthopedics and ambulatory imaging.

## Management concern

Management is evaluating how a 7% reimbursement reduction would affect operating margin, service-line throughput, patient access, and lender sentiment over the next 30, 90, and 180 days.
